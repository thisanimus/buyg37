About

Infiniti G37s 6MT Coupe

This car is the perfect balance of sporty and classy. It's not mind-bendingly fast, but it will put you back into your seat when you tell it to. The naturally aspirated 3.7L V6 is not fussy, and wants to be pushed. The shifter is short, heavy, and responsive; shifts are direct, slotting positively into gear. The ride is incredibly stable thanks to the near 50/50 weight distribution, and surprisingly comfortable given its stiffened suspension and larger sport wheels. The interior is understated and sophisticated, with leather seats and all of the luxury add-ons 2008 had to offer. While driving, the interior ambiance is pleasantly subdued, though the fantastic exhaust note's throaty growl settles into the cabin, reminding you of the G's Z-car heritage.
